A Revolution in Global Trade Logistics

Meridian Logistics is pioneering a revolution in global trade logistics by leveraging
advanced AI and blockchain technology to create a more transparent, efficient, and
resilient supply chain network.

Our platform connects producers, manufacturers, distributors, and end-users through a
unified digital ecosystem that provides real-time visibility, predictive analytics, and
automated compliance management.

The global trade logistics market is valued at $18.4 trillion and is projected to grow
at a CAGR of 7.2% through 2030. Traditional logistics systems are plagued by
inefficiencies, lack of transparency, and manual processes that cost the industry
$1.5 trillion annually in operational waste.

Meridian Logistics addresses these challenges head-on with our proprietary AI-driven
platform that provides end-to-end supply chain visibility, predictive risk assessment,
and automated compliance management.

Our solution targets the $4.2 trillion freight forwarding market and the $2.1 trillion
customs brokerage services market, which are ripe for digital transformation.

Meridian Logistics was founded by a team of supply chain veterans, AI specialists,
and blockchain innovators who recognized the need for a more intelligent logistics
ecosystem.

Our leadership team has over 50 years of combined experience in logistics technology,
supply chain management, and global trade compliance.

We have assembled a world-class advisory board comprising industry leaders from
Fortune 500 companies, logistics technology providers, and blockchain pioneers.

Meridian Logistics is headquartered in New York with operations spanning North
America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific regions.

Our global footprint allows us to serve clients across multiple time zones and
provide localized support while maintaining centralized oversight.

Technical Architecture

Meridian Logistics is built on a modern, scalable architecture designed for
reliability, security, and performance.

Core Components

Microservices Architecture: Modular microservices architecture enabling
independent scaling and development of different platform components.

Event-Driven Design: Event-driven architecture for real-time processing and
coordination of supply chain events.

Containerization: Docker containers for consistent deployment and scaling
across different environments.

Orchestration: Kubernetes orchestration for container management, scaling,
and high availability.

Data Architecture

Multi-Database Architecture: Polyglot persistence with separate databases for
different data types (time-series for tracking, graph for relationships,
document for unstructured data).

Data Streaming: Apache Kafka for real-time data streaming and event processing.

Data Lake: Data lake for storing raw supply chain data and supporting
advanced analytics and machine learning.

Caching Layer: Redis for high-performance caching of frequently accessed data
and reducing latency.

Security Architecture

Zero Trust Security: Zero-trust security model with continuous verification
of all users, systems, and devices.

Encryption: End-to-end encryption for data at rest and in transit using industry-
standard protocols.

Identity Management: Advanced identity and access management with role-based
access control and multi-factor authentication.

Compliance Framework: Built-in compliance monitoring and reporting for
industry regulations and standards.
